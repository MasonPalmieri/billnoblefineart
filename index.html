About

Bill Noble is a New England painter whose work explores coastal light and atmosphere throughout Cape Cod.

His work has been exhibited at the Cahoon Museum of American Art, Highfield Hall, Attleboro Art Museum and Cape Cod Cultural Center.

Bill teaches oil painting at the Falmouth Art Center where he is a lifetime member and former board member.
